About the Doctor

Your Health is Our Priority

Dr. Aarti Sharma is a highly qualified and experienced physiotherapist with over 10 years of dedicated practice. Her patient-first philosophy — "Because Every Body Deserves Care" — is the foundation of everything she does at the clinic.

She specialises in a broad spectrum of conditions including neurological rehabilitation, musculoskeletal disorders, post-operative recovery, and geriatric care. Her approach combines evidence-based techniques with personalised attention to achieve the best outcomes for each patient.

The clinic is conveniently located at Tower-21, 1904, Nirala Estate-2, and Dr. Aarti also offers home visits for patients who require care in the comfort of their own home.
